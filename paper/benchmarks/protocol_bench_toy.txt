  Notes:
  - Timing uses toy model (dim=16). Real models scale with dim².
  - Size projections assume N=1000 tokens, k challenged.
  - Commitment is always 68 bytes (two 32-byte roots + u32 count).
  - Verifier cost is O(k) — independent of N.
  - Proof size is O(k · trace_per_token + k · log₂N · 64).
  - Compact format eliminates x_ffn and h (derivable by verifier).
  - zstd ratio measured ~20% on toy model; conservative for projections.
